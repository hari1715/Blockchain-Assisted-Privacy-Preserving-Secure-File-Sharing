FAQs
How do I upload a file securely?

Select a receiver, choose your file and submit. An OTP is sent to verify the upload, and a SHA-256 hash is recorded for file integrity.

Why is face verification required?

It is the second authentication layer — it confirms the registered user is accessing the account even if the password is compromised.

OTP not received?

Check your inbox and spam folder. Ensure the email used during registration is correct. Try requesting again after a few minutes.

How does blockchain improve security?

Every login, upload, and download event is recorded as an immutable blockchain transaction — creating a tamper-proof audit trail.

Unable to download a shared file?

The sender must authorise your account as the receiver. OTP verification must also be completed before the download is allowed.

How It Works
1Register with your details and enrol your face via the camera
2Log in using password + facial biometric verification
3Sender selects a receiver, uploads a file and enters the OTP
4Receiver requests the file, completes OTP, and downloads securely
5All events are logged to the blockchain ledger automatically
